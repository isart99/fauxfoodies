Taco Hotspot

THE taco spot, really! High heels are not recommended to dine here, as there are no chairs in this restaurant. With excellent locations, staff, vibe, and food, Los Tacos No. 1 is sure to rank first on your taco list, as it does on ours.

The weekly ranking

  1. Ichiran
  2. Ippudo/Tabetomo
  3. Ivan ramen
  4. Mr. Taka
  5. E.A.K.

Ichiran is the complete ramen experience. It sticks to its roots and sits costumers in individual booths to enjoy an intimate journey full of flavors and noodle refills upon request! Ippudo is a worthy second, although this may raise some eyebrows. If you didn't know, Ippudo and Ichiran are the two ramen teams people in Japan choose from. Sorry team Ippudo. Ivan has a great vegan option, you don't want to miss out on one of the most flavorful tomatoes of your life. Mr. Taka's smokey meat has caused much debate among our writers about where to rank it, some argue it belongs higher up! E.A.K. is another good vegan option with plenty of mushrooms as toppings.
